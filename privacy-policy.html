This information is collected by Google AdMob in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

IMPORTANT: We do NOT collect, upload, or transmit your photos or videos.

When you make in-app purchases, Apple StoreKit processes the transaction. We receive transaction confirmation and subscription status. This information is handled by Apple in accordance with Apple's Privacy Policy.
